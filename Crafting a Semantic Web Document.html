DIY: How to replace your alternator(2004 Infiniti FX35)

Contact

Email: curtisjpierce39@gmail.com

LinkedIn: Curtis J (CJ) Pierce

Steps:

  1. Disconnect negative battery cable
  2. Remove alternator under bracket (12 mm)
  3. Loosen belt tensioner bolt(12mm)
  4. Loosen belt tensioner pulley bolt(14 mm)
  5. Remove serpentine belt
  6. Disconnect negative wire bolt (10 mm)
  7. Disconnect wire harness (12 mm)
  8. Remove alternator
  9. Reverse process to install new Alternator
Necessary Tools
Ratchet
10mm, 12mm, 14mm sockets
Ratchet extension
Mini Screwdriver
