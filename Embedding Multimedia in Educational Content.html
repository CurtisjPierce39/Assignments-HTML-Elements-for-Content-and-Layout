The 7 Steps of the Water Cycle

What are the 7 steps of the water cycle?

  1. Evaporation:

    2. Liquid surface water is heated by the sun and evaporates into atmospheric water vapor.

  2. Condensation:

    3. Water vapor molecules come together and cool, forming liquid water.

  3. Precipitation:

    4. Water returns from the atmosphere to the surface as rain, sleet, snow, hail, etc.

  4. Sublimation:

    5. Precipitation, such as snow or frozen water, instantaneously changes from ice to gaseous water vapor.

  5. Transpiration:

    6. Liquid water within plants is heated by the sun and evaporates through openings in their leaves, called stomata.

  6. Runoff:

    7. Gravity pulls liquid water in a flow of precipitation over the ground.

  7. Infiltration:

    8. Liquid water percolates down through the soil into the ground.
